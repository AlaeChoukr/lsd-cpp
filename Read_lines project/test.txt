Maxime Vachier-Lagrave (French pronunciation: ​[maksim vaʃje laɡʁav]; born 21 October 1990), often referred to by his initials, MVL, is a French chess grandmaster who 
is the reigning World Blitz Chess Champion. With a peak rating of 2819, Vachier-Lagrave is the seventh-highest rated player in history. A chess prodigy, Vachier-
Lagrave earned the title of grandmaster in 2005, at age 14.
Since 2007, the English Wikipedia page of Maxime Vachier-Lagrave has received more than 610,611 page views. His biography is available in 31 different languages on
Wikipedia. Maxime Vachier-Lagrave is the 224th most popular chess player (up from 226th in 2019), the 4,938th most popular biography from France (down from 4,435th in 
2019) and the 6th most popular French Chess Player.
